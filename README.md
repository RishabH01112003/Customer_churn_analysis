# Customer_churn_analysis


A dynamic and interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer churn patterns, service usage and demographic behavior  enabling telecom businesses to understand customer attrition and improve retention strategies.



##  Purpose

The Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard helps analysts and managers explore customer retention performance and churn drivers across multiple dimensions such as contract type, internet service, age group and payment method.  
It provides a unified view of churn rate, customer demographics and service behaviors for supporting data-driven retention and marketing decisions.



##  Business Problem

Customer churn poses a major challenge in the telecom industry, leading to significant revenue loss and unstable customer growth.  
The goal was to identify patterns, predict risk factors and visualize churn behavior effectively — allowing businesses to understand the underlying causes of attrition and take proactive measures to retain customers.



##  Key KPIs

- **Total Customers** – Total number of active users.  
- **Total Churn** – Number of customers who discontinued the service.  
- **New Joiners** – Total number of new customers in the selected period.  
- **Churn Rate** – Percentage of customers lost.  



##  Dashboard Features

- **Overall KPIs (Card Visuals):** Displays churn and customer metrics for a quick overview.  
- **Churn by Gender (Donut Chart):** Visualizes churn distribution between male and female customers.  
- **Churn by Age Group (Line and Stacked Bar Chart):** Highlights churn variations across different age brackets.  
- **Churn by State (Map/Column Chart):** Shows regions with the highest churn percentages.  
- **Churn by Payment Method (Bar Chart):** Compares churn across different billing options.  
- **Churn by Contract Type (Column Chart):** Illustrates churn based on contract duration.  
- **Churn by Tenure (Line and Stacked Bar Chart):** Analyzes how churn rates change with customer tenure.  
- **Churn by Internet Type (Bar Chart):** Displays churn rates among fiber, DSL and other connection types.  
- **Churn Category & Reason (Bar Chart):** Highlights top churn causes such as dissatisfaction, competition, and pricing.  
- **Churn by Services Used (Matrix Table):** Shows churn behavior among customers with diffrent services being used. 



##  Insights & Business Impact

- The **overall churn rate is 27.4%** indicating potential to enhance retention strategies.  
- **Month-to-month contract holders** are the most churn-prone, emphasizing the need for long-term contracts.  
- **Fiber optic users** show the highest churn, suggesting potential pricing or service issues.  
- Customers aged **50+** tend to churn more, possibly due to service complexity or affordability.  
- Absence of **add-on services** like online security or backup increases churn risk.  
- **Mailed check** and **bank withdrawal** payment users exhibit higher churn tendencies.  
- Encouraging **annual plans, bundled offers and loyalty programs** can reduce churn and improve retention.  

This dashboard empowers telecom decision-makers to identify at-risk customers, uncover churn trends, and design data-driven retention strategies that enhance long-term customer relationships and business stability.
